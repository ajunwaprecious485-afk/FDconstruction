How Pick and Pack Works

A streamlined process designed for speed and accuracy at every step.

1. Order Receives

Orders flow in from your store — Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon, or API — and are instantly routed to the nearest warehouse.

2. Smart Picking

Our system optimizes pick paths to minimize travel time. Barcode scanning ensures the right product every time.

3. Professional Packing

Products are packed in appropriate packaging — poly mailers, corrugated boxes, or custom branded packaging.

4. Labeling & Weighing

Each package is labeled, weighed for accurate shipping, and scanned into the carrier's system.

5. Carrier Dispatch

Shipped via UPS, FedEx, or USPS with real-time tracking sent to you and your customer.
