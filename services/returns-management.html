Returns Lifecycle

A systematic approach to processing returns quickly and efficiently.

1

Receive Return

Returned items arrive at our warehouse and are logged into our returns management system.

2

Inspect & Grade

Each item is inspected for condition, completeness, and functionality - graded A through D.

3

Disposition

Items are routed to restock, refurbish, liquidate, or dispose based on condition and your rules.

4

Update Inventory

Inventory counts are adjusted, restocked items are available for sale, and data is synced to your store.

Disposition Options

You control what happens to every returned item.

Restock

Grade-A items are cleaned, repackaged, and returned to sellable inventory immediately.

Refurbish

Minor damage repaired, components replaced, and items tested before restocking.

Liquidate

Unsellable inventory is routed to liquidation channels to recover residual value.

Recycle / Donate

Environmentally responsible disposal or charity donation with tax documentation.

Return to Vendor

Coordinate RMA with suppliers for warranty returns or vendor credit recovery.

Analytics & Reporting

Detailed return reason codes, trend analysis, and actionable insights to reduce return rates.
